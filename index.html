I Wanna Get Technical, Technical

What we offer is a nifty little device that you can place on your washing machine. This doodad will sense when the unit is doing the boogie-AKA the spin cycle. After everything has splished, splashed, and rinsed, that little robot that you affixed to your washer realizes that there's nothing happening anymore! This is information you need to know ASAFP. That way, you can pause your Netflix binge and head on down to the laundry room, past that weird, hanging wire, around the puddle of whoknowswhat, and fish out your mismatching socks to continue the cycle of cycles. As soon as you have the hardware and the software, we can start to send you emails about your underwear! ... Or at least when the washer is done... it may or may not have underwear, we don't really want to know.
